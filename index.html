Expert appraisals

IllinoisAppraisal is a real estate appraisal service for anyone who would benefit from the services of an Illinois State Certified Real Estate Appraiser.

Henry M. Jung, an Illinois State Certified Real Estate Appraiser located in Chicago, Illinois, operates IllinoisAppraisal.Com. Henry has 20 years of experience appraising real estate property in Chicago, and across Cook, Lake, Grundy, McHenry, DuPage, Will and Kane Counties.

A real estate appraisal is a valuable tool for anyone who needs to know the current market value for a specific property. If you own a single-family residence, 2-4-unit building, multi-family income building, condominium, or vacant land, and need a market valuation today... you have found the place!

What we can do for you

Appraisal services for: PMI removal, estate and trusts, divorce, loans and mortgages, tax appeal, insurance replacement cost, retroactive appraisals, IRS requirements, relocations, bail bonds, credit unions, and estimating market value for possible sale; also called prelisting.

Privately held valuation and consulting firm providing insightful, accurate, and timely valuation services of commercial and residential real estate assets for lenders, creditors, property owners, investors and portfolio & asset managers.

We focus on identifying current asset value, increasing existing value, decreasing expense ratios, augmenting revenue, maximizing income and/or managing liability and exposure on current assets & proposed investments and developments.

In today's tumultuous real estate markets, whether identifying distressed investments or valuing deteriorating assets, liability management and containment is paramount to future success. Our clients consistently engage Illinois Appraisal to produce superior results as a valued member of their team.

Our team performs real estate appraisals and consulting services, provides expert testimony, fraud research and analysis, partial interest valuation, forensic reviews, litigation support, as well as tax appeal valuation and sale-lease back analysis with market based transfer pricing.

For residential property owners, Henry assists with quantifying the impact of distressed properties (foreclosures, short sales, REO/bank owned and estate sale) on a neighborhood, pre-listing appraisals to determine ability to sell and pricing strategies, estate sale valuation for estate settlement, wealth transfer valuation of assets (including partial interest), and proposed remodeling/construction valuation.

In summary, Illinois Appraisal approaches each client assignment as a trusted adviser and delivers expert results with high customer satisfaction.
